Cost Recovery Study & Implementation

Role: Lead partner / internal project lead • Focus: Service area, staff training, executive presentation, implementation plan

What I did

  • Helped lead a comprehensive cost recovery study to determine the department’s service area and classify services by public vs. private benefit.
  • Trained staff on the cost recovery model, translating policy into day-to-day pricing and programming decisions.
  • Presented findings to local officials and stakeholders, aligning expectations across Council, leadership, and community partners.
  • Designed the implementation plan with phasing, communications, and change-management steps for sustainable adoption.

Impact

  • Established a clear pricing philosophy tied to benefit tiers and equity goals.
  • Created a repeatable framework for annual fee review and budget planning.
